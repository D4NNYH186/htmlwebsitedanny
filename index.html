About Me

My name is Daniel Heeney, I am a vocalist and artist with an invested interest in technology and web/game design. I have been a bit of a doodler ever since the age of 5, but started taking art a little more seriously when I had a brief stint as a tattoo apprentice in early adulthood. Singing has been an interest of mine since a 4yr old singing in the mirror and I have frequented various bands throughout my life, also featuring on Popstars: The Rivals and The Voice. My interest in gaming stems from my chchildhood, when my father bought me an Amstrad CPC 464 , then progressing on to a mega drive and further console gaming as the years passed.


I developed an interest in coding and web design whilst spending time with a few friends that already made the jump into the tech/developing sector and industry. I then proceeded to change careers to find a position with better working hours that would allow me to code in the evenings, I used various websites like freecodecamp.org and Treehouse, working on some of the syllabus on there.


From then, I searched for various opportunities to take coding further, extend my knowledge of code and pick up the skills and knowledge to enter in to the industry and sectors that I would like to work in. I came across an advertisement for codeNation on instagram, when I looked into this and spoke with the staff at the school, this seemed like a great opportunity that I could not pass up, jumped at the chance to join the course and started as soon as I possibly could. I am hoping to gain an apprenticeship and continue my learning on the 3 month master coding course.
