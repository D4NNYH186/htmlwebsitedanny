About Me

My name is Daniel Heeney, I am a vocalist and artist with an invested interest in technology and web/game design. Since the age of 10 I have been quite the doodler,
but started taking art a little more seriously when I had a brief stint as a tattoo apprentice. Singing has been an interest of mine since a 4yr old singing in the
mirror and have frequented various bands throughout my life, also featuring on Popstars: The Rivals and The Voice. My interest in gaming stems from my childhood
when my father purchased an Amstrad CPC 464 for me, progressing on to console gaming as the years passed.


I developed an interest in coding and web design whilst spending time with a few friends that already made the jump into the tech/developing sector and industry,
I then proceeded to change careers to find a position with better working hours that would allow me to code in the evenings, I used various websites like freecodecamp.org,
working on some of the syllabus on there.


From then, I searched for various opportunities to take coding further and extend my knowledge of code and pick up the skills and knowledge to enter in to the industry
and sectors that I would like to work in. I came across an advertisement for codeNation on instagram, when I looked into this and spoke with the staff at the school,
this seemed like a great opportunity that I could not pass up, jumped at the chance to join the course and started as soon as I possibly could. I am hoping to gain
an apprenticeship and continue my learning on the 3 month master coding course.
